Dylan Construction offers a wide range of building services including extensions, alterations, structural work, refurbishment, garden rooms/office's, cellar and loft conversions both commercial and residential using trusted plumbing and heating engineers, electricians, plasterers and roofers that we work alongside of.

When you are ready to transform your property, you need someone you can trust, a professional team that know what they are doing and will provide you with great quality work and an excellent customer service experience.

Alterations

We are specialists at altering all types of homes and buildings including commercial property, the majority of Dylan Constructions projects by far involve renovating and altering both period and modern properties in the residential sector, we do both.

Structural Work

We do structural steel work, calculated design all supplied and installed.

Cellar/Loft Conversions

We appreciate and understand that a loft conversion is probably going to be your second biggest investment, therefore, it is critical you choose the right loft company, Dylan Construction offer a second to none service and we involve you in every step of the way through the construction. We offer a full service from plans to completion so you can relax and know your conversion is being constructed by a company with vast experience in the loft conversion industry.

Refurbishments

We are able to manage all throughout the entire design and build process, our service can be fully comprehensive, covering everything from your initial design ideas right through to the completion of your project.

Garden Rooms/Offices
